# Public language policy

This public release is English-only.

The scientific core, specifications, review instructions, issue templates, website copy, and public communication materials are supplied in English. Earlier internal and blind-review artifacts may have been produced in Russian. Where those artifacts are not redistributed, their exact SHA-256 identities remain recorded in the review evidence.

The English public testing packet is a translation and publication derivative. It must not be described as byte-identical to the packet used in the sixth blind model review.
