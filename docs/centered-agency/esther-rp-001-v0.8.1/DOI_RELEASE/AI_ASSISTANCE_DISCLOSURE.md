# AI assistance disclosure

Ivan Kotov directed the research programme, selected the questions, approved the
claim boundaries, and remains responsible for the published work.

AI systems assisted with drafting, translation, formalization, code generation,
test generation, adversarial review, reproducibility packaging, and editorial
assembly. Machine-generated reviews were treated as bounded evidence and were
repeatedly challenged through independent blind runs and executable
counterexamples.

The final public release explicitly does not treat machine review as human peer
review. Human conceptual review and an external endpoint-rater pilot remain open
requirements before a registered matched-control study.
