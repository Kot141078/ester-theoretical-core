# v0.8-D - Sealed Measurement and Statistical Contract

This surface is carried forward from `v0.7` and remains sealed by digest registries.

## Assignment identity

The canonical digest includes assignment, block, arm, task, ordered opportunities, oracle-unresolvable metadata, and scoring configuration. One identifier cannot denote two different denominators.

## Analysis plan

The canonical digest includes exact `SRR/OCA/PAR/RCG`, block registry, margins, family alpha, tail rule, catastrophic margin, scoring configuration, and software profile. The expected digest comes from outside the analysed data.

## Independent implementation

The secondary path independently validates identity, ranges, universes, and tails. Six frozen fixtures are compared at field level. This is a software cross-check, not an independent scientific replication.

## Behavioural boundary

Primary endpoints are behavioural proxies. They do not prove a hidden mechanism. Mechanism claims require conformance gates or interventions.

## Acceptance criteria

```text
changed assignment content is rejected
changed plan content is rejected
invalid raw contracts are rejected by both paths
the same block and tail universes are required
six valid fixtures agree
```
