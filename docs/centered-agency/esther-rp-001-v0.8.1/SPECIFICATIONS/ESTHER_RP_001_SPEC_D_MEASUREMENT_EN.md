# v0.8-D — Sealed Measurement and Statistical Contract

## Assignment identity

The canonical digest covers assignment, block, arm, task, ordered opportunities, oracle-unresolvable metadata, and scoring configuration. One ID cannot denote two different denominators.

## Analysis plan

The canonical digest covers the exact `SRR/OCA/PAR/RCG` set, block registry, margins, family alpha, tail rule, catastrophic margin, scoring configuration, and software profile. The expected digest comes from outside the analysed data.

## Independent implementation

The secondary path independently validates identity, ranges, universes, and tails. Six frozen fixtures are compared at field level, but this remains a software cross-check rather than an independent scientific replication.

## Behavioural boundary

The primary endpoints are behavioural proxies. They do not prove a hidden mechanism. Mechanism claims require conformance gates or interventions.
